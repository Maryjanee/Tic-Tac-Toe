## Tic-Tac-Toe Game
This application is a command line implementation of the  classic Tic tac toe game.
Tic-tac-toe (American English), noughts and crosses (British English), is a game for two players, and each of the players are assigned tags(X and O) ,each player take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid with their assigned tag. The player who succeeds in placing three of their tag in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

In order to win the game, a player must place three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
